To enter Podcast Transfer Mode:
  1. Connect the RØDECaster Pro via USB
  2. On its touchscreen, tap the SD card icon
  3. Enable Podcast Transfer Mode

Sound pad upload isn't supported in this app.

Loading sounds onto the pads requires RØDE's proprietary USB protocol, which this app does not implement. You can still record to and manage sound pads directly on the RØDECaster Pro itself.

This app focuses on getting your recordings off the device — see the Podcasts tab. The device readout below just confirms your RØDECaster is connected.
